Hero scroll-scrub wires now anchor to the registry rail's outer edge with index-fanned Y on desktop (was tracking interior row rect, which dragged with rail-list internal auto-scroll). DESIGN.md updated with a Differentiation Guardrails section (testable rules: banned visual combination, allowed light bg HEX ranges, Fraunces deprecated for landing, hover-press-down mandatory, arc-blue restricted role, pre-merge screenshot requirement) and Material & Print Vocabulary section, and asserted as the canonical source of truth (implementations follow DESIGN.md, not the reverse).
